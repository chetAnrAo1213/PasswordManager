How Does AES Encryption work?

AES in GCM mode with a static secret key and IV. The secret key and IV are base64-encoded strings that are decoded during initialization ensuring encryption integrity through tag length of 128 bits. The reverse mechanism of the AES will gives you the decrypted password.
